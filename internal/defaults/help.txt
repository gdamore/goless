Navigation
Up/Down, j/k: move
Left/Right, h/l: scroll horizontally
PgUp/PgDn, b/f, Space: page
g/G: top/bottom
w: toggle wrap

Search
/: search forward
?: search backward
n/N: next/previous match
F2: cycle search mode (smart/case/nocase)
F3: toggle substring/whole-word matching

Commands
:123: jump to line 123
:set searchcase smart|case|nocase
:set searchword sub|word

General
H or F1: help
q or Esc: quit
